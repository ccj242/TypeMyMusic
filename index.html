TypeMyMusic is the best, free, music composing font on the web.

Compose scores, even ones with a grand staff, in your favorite text-editor of choice. Ideal for hobbyists and students alike looking for a free, easy to learn, easy to use alternative to Sibelius and Finale. Developed by Christopher Cinq-Mars Jarvis and music teacher Jocelyn Kraus.
